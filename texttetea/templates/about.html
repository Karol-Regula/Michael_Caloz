    About TETEA

    Mission Statement

    “To empower Tanzanians through education.”

    We work to achieve our mission by expanding educational opportunities in Tanzania through sponsoring needy students and working to develop learning centers in the country.

    TETEA was founded in November 2008 and is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.

    About this Site

    This site is designed from the TETEA text service. The site was written for a senior level computer science elective at Stuyvesant High School in New York City.

    Usage Instructions

    Select your desired subject and type from the dropdown menus!
    If you choose questions, you are given quiz sets so you can test your knowledge. With notes, simply pick a specific topic on interest. If definitions are chosen, you are able to select either a letter, where you can then search for a word you are looking for, or a random set of definitions, which function as flashcards to quiz yourself. Press submit when you're ready to retrieve your information.
